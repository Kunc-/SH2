Sacred Heart Scavenger Hunt

Year 6, welcome to the ultimate (ish) scavenger hunt. DO NOT PRESS THE START BUTTON UNTIL YOU ARE TOLD TO, or your time will start early.

Instructions

  • When instructed, press the start button. You will be taken to this page, and the timer will start:
  • Each item has a set of instructions, a number of points and a box for the code to be entered in.
  • Codes are random five number (positive) sequences, such as 12345 or 00000.
  • Given that the winner will receive prizes, you should not share your codes with anyone else. It's also unsporting.
