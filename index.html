Sacred Heart Scavenger Hunt

Year 6, welcome to the ultimate (ish) scavenger hunt. DO NOT PRESS THE START BUTTON UNTIL YOU ARE TOLD TO, or your time will start early.

Instructions/Rules

  • When instructed, press the start button. You will be taken to this page, and the timer will start:
  • Each item has a riddle, a number of points and a box for the code to be entered in.
  • Codes are random five number (positive) sequences, such as 12345 or 00000.
  • Entry of the correct code in to the correct riddle will earn you the stated number of points.
  • When your time expires, return to Year 6. The program will not accept entries after that time.
  • NO RUNNING (especially on slippery surfaces)
  • You should not share your codes with anyone outside your team. It's unsporting.
  • You should not move codes - anyone doing so will be disqualified.
  • No Bull-dogging (following one team to find all their codes).

Restrictions on Code Placement

  • There are no codes anywhere that you'd have to go through rain to get - please don't get yourself soaked
  • There are no codes outside the school, or in bathrooms.
  • There are no codes that require you to enter any buildings.
  • There may be codes hidden in rooms that you aren't allowed to go into, but which are easily visible from the outside.
  • There are no codes in stupid places, such as in gardens, on roofs, or in cars.
